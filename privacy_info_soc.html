What the digital labs do

The interactive labs and workbooks in this course run entirely in your web browser. When you type a response or answer a question, your work is saved to that device automatically. Nothing leaves your browser until you click Submit.

When you do submit, two things happen: your score is sent directly to the Canvas gradebook, and a record is logged in a secure spreadsheet. Neither contains your full name — only your CN email address, your section, and your score.

🤖
How AI feedback works

Several questions offer an optional Get Feedback button. When you click it, your typed response is sent to an AI assistant (Claude, made by Anthropic) which reads your answer and replies with a short, encouraging note.

Here is exactly what the AI receives:

  • Your written response text only — the words you typed in that one box
  • The question context, so the AI knows what you were asked
  • Nothing else — no name, no email, no grade, no section
Important The AI cannot identify you. It sees anonymous text and responds to it. Your response is not stored by Anthropic beyond the single request.
🔒
What is and is not shared
Stays in Canvas Your grade, your identity, your enrollment — all managed by Canvas, which is FERPA-compliant.
Stays on your device All your in-progress responses are saved locally until you submit. Clearing your browser history will erase them.
Never sent to AI Your name, email, student ID, section, or score are never included in any AI request.
Never sold or shared Your responses are not used by this course for any purpose other than providing feedback on your work.
✍
You can opt out — no penalty

If you are not comfortable with the digital submission or AI feedback features, you are welcome to complete the lab in the browser as normal and submit screenshots instead of using the Submit button. Your grade will not be affected in any way.

To opt out, follow these steps:

  1. 1 Complete the lab as normal in your browser. Your progress saves automatically as long as you use the same device.
  2. 2 Take screenshots of every completed section.
  3. 3 Do not click the Submit button inside the lab.
  4. 4 Upload all screenshots directly to the Canvas assignment by the posted deadline.
Note on saving Your lab progress saves automatically to the device and browser you are using. If you switch devices or clear your browser data, your work will not carry over. Stick to the same browser on the same device until you are ready to submit or screenshot.
