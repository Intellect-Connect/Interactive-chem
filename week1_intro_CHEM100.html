Section IV

Common Questions

Do I really need to buy materials this week?
Yes. Lab 1 begins Week 2 (March 16) and you need materials ready before then. Most items are already in your kitchen and the total cost is only $15–25 for the full course.
Can I use substitutions for lab materials?
Yes. The checklist includes substitution options for each item. If you substitute, document what you used in your lab reflection — it will not affect your grade as long as you explain your reasoning.
What exactly is Bracket City?
Bracket City is the fictional community that frames all of your chemistry work. You are not completing separate “Bracket City assignments” — your labs, workbooks, and discussions are the investigation. Each lab provides evidence that builds toward your final case file.
I have not done chemistry before. Will I be able to keep up?
Yes. This course is designed for students with no prior chemistry background. Concepts are introduced through real-world food safety problems so you always understand the “why” before the “how.” The chapter videos and guided notes are your foundation — watch them before each lab.
